Atamisque Experience

During the Atamisque experience, we’ll travel to the lands of Atamisque, a vineyard located in Valle de Uco, in the San José district of Tupungato. Along Provincial Route 86, also known as the Wine Route, you’ll spot the “Atamisque” estate.

What Will This Tasting Be Like?

This tasting will feature 5 wines from this winery, which include:

ATAMISQUE ASSEMBLAGE

The iconic wine of Atamisque Winery. The word “assemblage” means “blend” in French.

ATAMISQUE CHARDONNAY

100% Chardonnay, made from grapes from a single, unique vineyard in Tupungato, Valle de Uco. Fermented and aged 100% in new French oak barrels for 10-12 months.

ATAMISQUE MALBEC

Made from Malbec grapes from old vineyards over 80 years old from various plots in Valle de Uco.

ATAMISQUE CABERNET SAUVIGNON

Made from grapes from a unique, proprietary vineyard in Tupungato, located at 1,300 meters above sea level.

ATAMISQUE PETIT

Made from grapes from vineyards in San José, Tupungato, located at 1,300 meters above sea level.

Event Location

The tasting will take place at the Antonia Vinos location, located at 4250 Valparaíso Avenue, Unit 3.
