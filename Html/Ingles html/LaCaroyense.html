La Caroyense Experience

In the La Caroyense experience, we will seek to travel to the lands of Colonia Caroya through this tasting of wines and various foods. Regarding the varieties cultivated in Colonia Caroya, it has 92 hectares of vineyards that provide part of the grapes used for production.

What will this tasting be like?

This tasting will feature 6 different types of wines from this winery, which will include:

CAROYENSE LAGRIMILLA

Chandon Extra Brut is the classic of Chandon. The best Chardonnay and Pinot Noir grapes come from our El Yaima and Caicayén farms (Uco Valley) located between 1000 and 1200 meters above sea level. With their sandy to loamy soils and marked limestone presence, they allow us to create a fresh, fruity, elegant, creamy, and balanced sparkling wine.

CAROYENSE LA CAROYA

It has an attractive dark red color with violet hues. Fruity with a predominance of red fruits like raspberries and cherries, with an elegant, velvety medium body, sweet and well-balanced tannins.

CAROYENSE TANNAT

The grapes are harvested and selected manually in the vineyard and transported to the winery in the early hours of the morning, where we start the production process. During tasting, we determine the time for racking, malolactic fermentation, filtration, and winter resting, with aging in barrels for 12 months. Aseptic bottling and resting in bottles for 6 months.

CAROYENSE PIU NATURE

With a yellow color and golden hues, with small fine bubbles and long persistence. Delicately fruity aroma with citrus notes, white fruits, and a hint of yeast. Fresh, rounded flavor with good complexity and prolonged duration on the palate.

CAROYENSE PIU MARSELAN

Elegant sparkling wine with an intense red color, with aromas of ripe fruits, raisins, and plums, smooth and silky with balanced acidity, structured, fresh, and delicate palate. It is recommended to accompany meals of character.

CAROYENSE PIU EXTRA BRUT

This elegant sparkling wine has a red color with reflections, with floral aromas of rose muscat and fruity notes. With hints of strawberry, blueberries, and blackberries. Smooth palate, balanced acidity, making it fresh and delicate. It is recommended to consume during meals.

Event Location

The tasting will take place at the Antonia Vinos location, located at Avenida Valparaíso number 4250, local number 3.
