La Rural Experience

At La Rural Winery, with each glass, we savor the history of Mendoza. Since 1885, this vineyard has witnessed the passage of time, cultivating grapes of exceptional quality that reflect the unique terroir of the region. Just like a journey through time, our tasting will transport you through the decades, discovering the essence of an unparalleled winemaking legacy.

What will this tasting be like?

This tasting will feature 5 sparkling wines from this winery, which will include:

SAN FELIPE TINTO

This typical Argentinian red blend results in a product with unique characteristics. Firstly, the Cabernet Sauvignon is aged in first-use American oak barrels for twelve months.

SAN FELIPE 12 UVAS

Bright plum ruby color. A pronounced and fruity aroma, where fresh red fruit stands out, tinged with smoky and vanilla notes from the oak contact characterizing the first perceptions. On the palate, its roundness and balance between acidity and a pleasant structure due to fluid, soft tannins surprise.

SAN FELIPE BLANCO 2015

Pale yellow with greenish hues. Discreet floral notes blended with soft contributions of white, citrus, and tropical fruits allow you to appreciate its fresh spirit in a good balance between acidity and sweetness. It is frank, unctuous, and round, with a medium-length finish on the palate.

SAN FELIPE ROSÉ DE MALBEC

Crystal clear pink with cherry and coral hues. The accents of fresh red fruits (sour cherry, strawberry) win the nose in a delicate aromatic duo formed by flowers and a slight spicy touch. Excellent balance between acidity and structure. The long and dry aftertaste leaves a pleasant sensation of freshness on the palate.

EXTRA BRUT 2015

Dry yellow with bright reflections. It features fine and persistent bubbles. The aromas remind of the fragrance of ripe white fruits, in harmony with subtle toasted notes. With good creaminess, it leaves a memory of freshness on the palate with a sweet profile.
