Chandon Experience

In the Baron B experience, through this tasting, we will feel the essence of Chandon, a vineyard founded in the Andes Mountains in 1959, where the essence of this winery has been preserved.

What will this tasting be like?

This tasting will feature 5 sparkling wines from this winery, which are:

CHANDON EXTRA BRUT

Chandon Extra Brut is the great classic of Chandon. The best Chardonnay and Pinot Noir grapes come from our El Yaima and Caicayén vineyards (Uco Valley), located between 1000 and 1200 meters above sea level. With their sandy to loamy soils and marked presence of limestone, they allow us to create a fresh, fruity, elegant, creamy, and balanced sparkling wine.

CHANDON APÉRITIF

Chandon Apéritif is the expression of Chandon's innovative style. Our winemakers' expertise is expressed in this artisanal maceration of oranges from sustainable farms in Entre Ríos, along with a selection of spices from different parts of the world.

CHANDON ROSÉ

Chandon Rosé is the perfect balance between freshness and creaminess, achieving a pleasant sweet sensation and a friendly, youthful palate.

CHANDON CUVÉE BRUT NATURE

This sparkling wine captivates with its exceptional blend of Chardonnay and Pinot Noir from high-altitude vineyards in Mendoza. Our Caicayén and Cepas del Plata vineyards are strategically located between 1200-1450 meters above sea level, with lower temperatures and an average thermal amplitude of 17.5°C, with sandy soils and areas of clay and silt. This allows the grapes to ripen more slowly, resulting in higher natural acidity, an optimal condition for perfecting the aromas and flavors of our sparkling wines.

CHANDON CUVÉE RÉSERVE BLANC DE BLANCS

With the ambition to create a blend with our best high-altitude Chardonnays, Cuvée Réserve Blanc de Blancs was born. The intensity of citrus and white fruits from the Chardonnay merges with the delicacy provided by the long resting period on specially selected yeasts, creating a broad and creamy sparkling wine.
