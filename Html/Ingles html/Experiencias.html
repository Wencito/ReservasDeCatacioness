Book your experience now and dive into a world of wine passion.

LOPEZ WINERY

Antonia Vinos invites you on a journey through time with the wines of Bodegas López. Join us for an exclusive tasting where you’ll try iconic labels that reflect over a century of winemaking tradition. Guided by our sommeliers, you’ll explore the nuances and complexity of wines like López Malbec, an Argentine classic, and Traful, a young, fresh wine representing the winery’s new generation. Learn about the López family history and their legacy of crafting high-quality wines.

SANTA JULIA WINERY

Antonia Vinos invites you to a one-of-a-kind wine tasting experience at Santa Julia Winery. Our expert sommeliers will guide you on a sensory journey through Santa Julia’s diverse and complex wines. Sample a curated selection, featuring the intense Malbecs of Altamira, elegant Chardonnays from Tupungato, and fresh Pinot Noirs from San Pablo. Each wine will be introduced in detail, exploring its unique characteristics, the terroir, and the winemaking process. This is a rare chance to appreciate the passion and expertise that define Santa Julia Winery.
