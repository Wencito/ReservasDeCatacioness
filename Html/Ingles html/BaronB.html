Baron B Experience

In the Baron B experience, through this tasting, we will immerse ourselves in the lands of Baron B, a vineyard with the best high-altitude terroirs, 1500 meters or more above sea level, to obtain grapes from cool climate areas.

What will this tasting be like?

This tasting will include 4 sparkling wines from this winery, which will be:

BARON B EXTRA BRUT

Represents the Baron B style in its purest form, always attractive and refined. Yellow color with light ocher tones. Expressive aromas of apricot and citrus fruits.
Notes of toasted bread and dried fruits. Fresh and creamy. Sensual and with great persistence.

BARON B BRUT NATURE

Represents the Baron B style with its intensity, complexity, elegance, and delicacy. Golden yellow color with green highlights. Mineral, floral aromas with subtle anise spice notes.
Fresh and unctuous on the palate, with vibrant creamy texture from prolonged contact with yeasts.

BARON B ROSÉ

Represents the Baron B style with its attractive personality, aromatic complexity, structure, and elegance on the palate. Pale pink with subtle salmon tones. Intense, refined aromas of red and black fruits. Crisp, lively palate that evolves into a great aromatic expression brought by Malbec.
Balanced, fresh, with great breadth and volume.

BARON B HÉRITAGE

Baron B Héritage is always a blend of exceptional vintages inspired by the vision of its creator, Baron Bertrand. A unique sparkling wine that, looking to the future, in each edition honors a man who left his legacy for posterity.

Event Location

The tasting will be held at the Antonia Wines store, located at Valparaíso Avenue number 4250, store number 3.
