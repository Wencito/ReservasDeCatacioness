Lopez Winery Experience

In the Lopez experience, we will travel through a tasting that brings us to the lands of a historic winery that has endured over time.

What will this tasting be like?

This tasting will feature 5 wines from this winery, which include:

FEDERICO LÓPEZ GRAN RESERVA

Cabernet Sauvignon-Merlot blend from old vineyards planted in 1940. Made in homage to Don Federico López, creator of our fine wines, and son of the winery's founder, with love from his children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren.

FEDERICO LÓPEZ JEREZ

An aged, smooth, and light aperitif with exquisite aroma and flavor, following a century-old tradition: made with varietals from Jerez de la Frontera and aged in oak soleras.

LÓPEZ MALBEC

An iconic Argentine wine, maintaining its quality since its creation in 1973 and becoming an indisputable classic. This harmonious and well-balanced Malbec is aged in large French oak barrels.

LÓPEZ DULCE NATURAL

Made from grapes from our unique vineyard in Tupungato, located at 1,300 meters above sea level.

RINCÓN FAMOSO ROSÉ

Exquisite, fresh, balanced, and delicate. Made with Torrontés Riojano, Moscatel, and Viognier grapes. Lower in alcohol, naturally sweeter. Enjoy it well-chilled at any time, perfect with sweet dishes or as an aperitif.

Event Location

The tasting will take place at Antonia Vinos, located at Avenida Valparaíso, number 4250, shop number 3.
